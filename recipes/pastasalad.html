Pasta Salad

Description

This recipe will make the most perfect pasta salad. Prep is quick and cooking is a breeze. After completing this recipe, the pasta salad can be placed in the fridge to cool it down.

Ingredients

  • Italian dressing
  • Tricolor pasta
  • Cubed tomatoes
  • Diced cucumber
  • Crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta until al dente or firm to bite.
  2. Drain the pasta and rinse under cool water until no longer hot.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooled pasta with the tomatoes, cucumbers, feta, and italian dressing.
  4. Mix the ingredients together until combined.
