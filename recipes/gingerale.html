Ginger Ale

Description

This recipe makes 8 servings of ginger ale. This drink is best served cold and can be kept in the fridge for up to two weeks.

Ingredients

  • Fresh ginger root
  • Sparkling water
  • Simple syrup or any sweetner
  • One lemon

Steps

  1. Grate the ginger root until you have about two tablespoons of ginger
  2. Juice the lemon and place that fluid in a cup
  3. In a blender, put the lemon juice and the ginger root together. Blend until smooth
  4. In a container, mix the blended liquid with the sparkling water and the simple syrup.
