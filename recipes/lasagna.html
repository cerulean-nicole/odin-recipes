Lasagna

Description

This recipe makes an 8 serving lasagna in about 3 hours. About half the time will be spent on prep and the other half on baking.

Ingredients

  • half pound ground beef
  • half pound ground por
  • diced onion
  • canned tomatos
  • noodles
  • cheese: parmesean, cottage cheese, and shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Cook the meat together in a skillet until it's browned and cooked thorougly.
  2. Add the diced onion to the skilled containing the meat, and continue cooking until the onion is translucent
  3. Add the tomato, parsley, garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and sugar to the skilled
  4. Cooked the noodles in salted water until it is al dente. Then set the noodles aside
  5. Mix the cheeses, eggs, parlsey, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  6. Assemble the lasagna by placing noodles in the baking pan first. Then layer the items between the sauce, cheese, and meat. Putting a new layer of noodles on top each time. Finish by placing the shredded mozzarella on top
  7. Cover the baking pan with foil and bake the lasagna for half an hour. After half an hour, remove the foil and continue baking the lasagna until the top is golden browm.
